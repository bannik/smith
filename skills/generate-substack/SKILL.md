# Generate Substack Newsletter

Weekly newsletter: **"The Week That Was (And Shouldn't Have Been)"**
Published Sundays. Recaps the week's biggest geopolitical stories with Audacity Timeline commentary.

## Structure

### Headline
"The Week That Was (And Shouldn't Have Been) — [Date Range]"

### Section 1: The Big One
The single biggest story of the week. 2-3 paragraphs. Explain what happened, why it matters, and what nobody's talking about. Voice: informed, dry, slightly exhausted by the state of the world.

### Section 2: Meanwhile...
3-4 stories that flew under the radar. One paragraph each. The "while you were watching X, Y happened" section.

### Section 3: The Audacity Award
The single most audacious thing a world leader, government, or institution did this week. Written with maximum "are you serious?" energy. This is the signature section — the one people share.

### Section 4: What to Watch
2-3 things happening next week that you should pay attention to. Brief, forward-looking.

### Sign-off
A one-liner. Dry. The mic drop.

## Voice

Same as Twitter but slightly longer-form. You can be more analytical here. The humor should emerge from the analysis, not be bolted on. Think John Oliver's writing but without the shouting.

## Process

1. Review `post-history.md` for the full week — what stories did we cover?
2. Review `self-reflection-log.md` — what resonated with the audience?
3. Web search for any stories we missed
4. Draft the newsletter
5. Save to `newsletters/[date]-weekly.md`

## Publishing

For now, save the draft as markdown. Manual publishing to Substack until API integration is set up.
