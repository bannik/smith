## Engagement Targets (Reply to their viral tweets)

These accounts regularly post tweets that go viral in the geopolitics niche. Our replies here get maximum eyeball exposure:

- @Reuters, @AP (massive reach, news wire)
- @BBCWorld, @CNN (mainstream, huge audiences)
- @FT, @TheEconomist (informed audience, our people)
- @elaboryan (Middle East / Gulf analysis)

## Rules

- Study primary references every 6 hours
- Reply to trending tweets from any of these accounts when they go viral
- Never copy their tweets — learn patterns, develop original voice
- Prioritize engaging with accounts that have 10k-100k followers (sweet spot for follow-backs)
- Don't engage with accounts currently in controversies or pile-ons
